Brymo

Artiste

Olawale Ashimi

Nigeria

  • Bio
  • Music
  • Videos
  • EP/Album

  • This is one fine artist Nigeria ever had in her music industry. The artiste who was formerly signed to Chocolate City is most notable for his distinguished voice. He's been lauded by many people for his unique voice, style of music and his amazing lyrics.

    He is the only child of a carpenter father and a petty trader mother. He was raised in Okokomaiko area of Lagos state.

    He attended Aganju aka primary school okokomaiko, lagos. 1990-1997 He moved on to Ajangbadi high school 1997-2003). Olawale gained admission into Lagos state university in 2007 where he studied zoology.

    In 2002, He started up Aliens with 3 other chaps. In a period of two years, he wrote over 85 songs. The chaps were together for some time until 2005 where Brymo went solo and recorded his first single in 2005. Not signed to any record label, he sought out the assistance of a local talent spotter, who helped him record his debut album titled Brymstone with Bujoc Records. In 2008, Brymo released a single entitled "Shawdy" along with a music video which made waves on various charts and music rating boards.

    Fortune smiled at him when he hooked up with Nigerian superstar rapper M.I in 2010. The rap boss MI saw the talent in him and facilitated his deal with record label Choc City immediately. Though he left the record label on some dissing level in 2013, he however released an album with them in 2012 called TheSonOfaKapenta .
    ARA, released in 2011 is Brymo’s 1st official single as an artiste under Chocolate City. ARA was the number 6 trending topic globally on twitter and the song rose to the top as the number 1 song in the country on various charts.

    He has been described as the master of hooks; this is because of his contribution to the success of Iceprince’s hit single Oleku’. He has featured on several tracks by other musicians, most notably Oleku by Ice Prince. This hit single spread like wild fire on air and also topped most charts in Nigeria and Sudan.

    Some of his notable achievements so far include:
