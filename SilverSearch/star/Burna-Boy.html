Burna Boy

Artiste

Damini Ogulu

Nigeria

  • Bio
  • Music
  • Videos
  • EP/Album

  • Burna Boy, born and raised in Ahaoda, River State is an artiste operating in the reggae-dance all terrain of Nigeria’s music industry.

    He was at Montessori International School in river state for elementary education and later moved to Lagos where he attended Corona Schools for his secondary education. Shortly afterwards, he left for the UK where he decided to chase his dreams in his newly discovered passion Music. His love and zeal for reggae is not unconnected to his Granddad’s musical link with the late Baba Fela.who used to be Fela’s Manager.

    The SpaceShip Entertainment CEO who headlined his own Industry Nite in 2012, was brought to limelight by his single "Like To Party" released earlier in the same year. He quickly shot the video directed by SqareBall media services.
